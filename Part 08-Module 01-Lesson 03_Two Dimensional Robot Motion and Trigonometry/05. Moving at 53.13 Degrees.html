05. Moving at 53.13 Degrees

Nd113 C6 L3 05 L Moving At 53 Degrees V2

The following questions are about a right triangle where \Theta = 53.13 ^{\circ}

You saw earlier that if D=5, then

\Delta x = 3

and

\Delta y = 4

If D = 10 meters then what would "Delta x" be?
SOLUTION: 6 meters

If D = 10 meters then what would "Delta y" be?
SOLUTION: 8

Please select the proper completion to the following sentence:

When driving at a heading of 53.13 degrees, the ratio of a car's vertical displacement to its total displacement will always equal __
SOLUTION: 4/5
