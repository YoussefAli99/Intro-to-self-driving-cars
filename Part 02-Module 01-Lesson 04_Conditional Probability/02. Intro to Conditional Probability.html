02. Intro to Conditional Probability

Introduction To Conditional Probability

What do you estimate is the probability P(cloudy) that one year from now it will be cloudy where you are now? ( P(cloudy) = 0.0 means "it definitely will not be cloudy" and P(cloudy) = 1.0 means it definitely will be cloudy).
SOLUTION: P(cloudy) < 0.05
