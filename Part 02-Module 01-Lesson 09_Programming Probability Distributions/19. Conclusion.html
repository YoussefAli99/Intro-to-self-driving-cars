19. Conclusion

Conclusion

In this lesson you've programmed a few discrete probability distribution including a continuous uniform distribution, a piece-wise uniform distribution, and a discrete distribution representing a mapped grid.

In the next section, you'll gain experience working with a very important continuous distribution called the Gaussian distribution. A Gaussian distribution is oftentimes used to represent uncertainty in sensor measurements or locations. Continue on to the next lesson to learn more.
